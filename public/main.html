INDIAN PREMIER LEAGUE

Overview

One of the key factors behind the IPL's success is its innovative franchise-based model. Unlike traditional cricket tournaments, where teams represent countries or regions, IPL teams are based in cities and are owned by a mix of industrialists, celebrities, and corporate entities. This model has not only added a competitive edge to the tournament but has also created a sense of local pride and identity among fans.
